Welcome Message from our Managing Director

Mango Tales Limited, is a publishing company started in 2006 in order to promote beauty, environmental consciousness, diversity, multilingualism, music appreciation and multisensory learning through children’s literature and music. It is my hope to make all our products as widely available as possible. We have converted HH to Spanish as well as are in the process of converting it to Braille and will continue working on making it available in different languages so that children the world over will be able to experience the magic of the tropics. We hope that this in itself will help children to celebrate diversity among people. We also hope to make our products as interactive as possible. Including content in musical, visual, audiovisual and even tactile formats will allow us to help teach students with different learning styles and abilities. The first book in the Mango Tales series is Hazel Hummingbird. It is a garden adventure for the whole family. Hazel starts her day in the waking garden, quickly flying here and there, making stops at her favourite flowers, bugs and bees, while children quietly gaze into the wonderful garden world. At the end of a good day, the children revel in the sunset and say goodbye so they can all settle down to rest.

Hazel Hummingbird: La Colibri Hazael, is written in English and Spanish, and vividly illustrated by Coretta Singer and Jerome Marshall. (include pictures?) The story gives the world a taste of Natallie’s experience growing up in Jamaica. Hazel Humminbird goes about her day in the beautiful garden world, working in perfect time with flowers, bugs and bees while Caribbean children gaze in the garden world or get up to their own fun.
