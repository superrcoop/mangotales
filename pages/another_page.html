Welcome Message from our Managing Director

Natallie Rochester

Mango Tales Limited, is a publishing company started in 2006 in order to promote beauty, environmental consciousness, diversity, multilingualism, music appreciation and multisensory learning through children’s literature and music. It is my hope to make all our products as widely available as possible. We have converted HH to Spanish as well as are in the process of converting it to Braille and will continue working on making it available in different languages so that children the world over will be able to experience the magic of the tropics. We hope that this in itself will help children to celebrate diversity among people. We also hope to make our products as interactive as possible. Including content in musical, visual, audiovisual and even tactile formats will allow us to help teach students with different learning styles and abilities.
